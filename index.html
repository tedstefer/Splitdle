Splitdle

Race through -dle games. Lock your splits. Beat your friends.

How It Works

1. Start

Click play and get dropped into a random -dle game.

2. Solve

Beat each game as fast as you can. Your splits are tracked automatically.

3. Compete

Share your result. Challenge friends. Climb the leaderboard.

Extension Required

To start a Splitdle run, you need the browser extension installed.
